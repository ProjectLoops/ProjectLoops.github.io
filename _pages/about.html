There are several ways of saving the planet. A lot of ways which we think can save the planet are wrong. We here present and work on different approach to this problem named PROJECT-LOOPS, where we try to loop the output of industrial production to input of the industries. This can't happen for free. So, we introduce 'Green Price', the actual price of the products.
